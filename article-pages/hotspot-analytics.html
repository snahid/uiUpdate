  • Radius Server – General Dashboard

    • Access point user tracking

    The Yourspot Dashboard gives you an overview of key metrics pertaining to the use of Free Wi-fi and the flow of visitors through your venue.

    The key sections of the dashboard are the map and Reports view.

    The map indicates the number of users connected to each Access Point at a particular moment in time. Being able to scroll through time gives you a clear indication of traffic patterns through the venue represented over any given time range.

    The report section provides a simple and user-friendly summarization of Wi-Fi connections and the insights they provide including:

    • The number of New VS Returning Visitors
    • The total number of Visitors at any given time
    • The average Peak Time of day for a particular period
    • Summary details for each access point
    • A list of top entrance and exit points to the venue
    • A list showing the average dwell time of visitors to each access point
